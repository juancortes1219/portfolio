Comex

This is the project that my team created for the semester, Comex. My team consisted of Abele Amsalu, Lauren James, Raheel Jooma, Camille Pascua, and myself. Community Exchange, Comex for short, is an app where neighbors can communicate about product availability in their dorm or apartment complex for exchanges. The goal is to eliminate inconveniences of store trips and instead have the person find the item by asking their neighbors.
