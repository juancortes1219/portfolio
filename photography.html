MY CAMERAS

Pentax K1000

This was my first camera. I bought it the fall of 2017. The reason I bought it was because a friend of mine had one. I was intrigued by its look. Back then, I wasn’t knowledgeable on cameras or even photography yet, so I had to do some bit of learning since the Pentax K1000 is a film camera.

Canon EOS Rebel T4i

This is my second camera. I bought this one spring of 2018. That semester at Georgia State University, I took “Intro to Photography”. I needed a DSLR for that class, so I went with the Rebel T4i since it was decent and affordable for a beginner camera. This is my main camera right now.
